Maiok E-commerce App Design

This is an APP I designed for my previous company, Maiok. The original goal of our e-commerce platform was to introduce local and global high-quality or organic products to our customers. At the same time, we hoped to increase customer loyalty with our products and our service. We achieved part of our goal but also encountered some problems.  According to our customer feedback, I found that Chinese consumers still have doubts about organic food or "green food" ( which is a kind of certification, issued by the Chinese government).
