Supporting the transition to a more plant-based diet

We proposed two different hypothesis to transit users to a more plant-based diet and tested with 9 people. They are people with relatively diverse backgroud.

  • Nine 30-min video interviews with friends and family
  • 3 participants are somewhat worried and 6 are very worried about climate change
  • Mix of age, gender, family type, and different levels of knowledge about the connection between food and the environment
  • Age groups: 25-40 and 45+
  • All participants are relatively mid-high income
  • Based out of 4 different states: Chicago, Ohio, Florida, Colorado
  • Concept Testing: participants spoke out loud while walking through two web-based concepts.
  • Followed by an in-depth discussion about their everyday habits and decision making criteria.
